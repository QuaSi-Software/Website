Coordination & development
siz energieplus acts as the project coordinator, both for internal communication between the partners as well as the larger open source community. We also develop and maintain the open source tools released as QuaSi Software. QuaSi grew as a toolbox from previous work for research projects on the energy systems of districts.
Digital Building Industries AG
DBI is a software company with offices in Esslingen, Böblingen, Oldenburg and Potsdam, on a mission to digitally transform the construction industry. To this end, DBI develops AI-powered SaaS solutions that make building planning, energy calculation and CO₂ optimization more productive and transparent.
