We have updated the website's design, moved some things around and added more content to the home page.

One big change is a new section that describes the organisations involved in the project and their role in it, as well as providing some related links. Another big change is the introduction of subpages for the various tools of the QuaSi toolbox and a carousel that briefly describes each.

We hope the changes help clarify for new readers what QuaSi is and who is behind the project.

<!--more-->

Further changes:
* The section on the organisations behind QuaSi on the contact page has been removed as this is now part of the home page
* The funding disclaimer has been moved from the contact page to the home page
* The contact page in general has been extended with more communication channels
* We have updated the imprint, the license file, the footer and the directories in the repository to clarify the exact license information of all assets that exist in the repository
* Content in the header and footer is now centered to be in line with the center-column-design of the content in general
* Two new entries in the header link to the "Partners" section on the home page and the tools subpages
